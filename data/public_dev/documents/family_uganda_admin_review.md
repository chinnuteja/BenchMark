# Family: Uganda-style Administrative Review Deadline Demo

This is a simplified, perturbed public-dev document family for evaluation research. It is not legal advice.

## Administrative Review Rule

### Demo Rule 10.1 — Filing Window

A party seeking review must file within 10 calendar days after receiving the Accounting Officer decision.

For this demo family, Day 1 is the day after receipt. The deadline expires at 23:59 local time on Day 10.

## Decision Timeline

The decision receipt date and filing date vary by benchmark item.
