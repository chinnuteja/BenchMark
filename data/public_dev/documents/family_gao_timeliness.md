## Bid Protest Rule

### 4 CFR §21.2(a)(1) — Apparent Solicitation Improprieties

A protest based on alleged improprieties in a solicitation that are apparent before the time set for receipt of initial proposals shall be filed before that time.

### 4 CFR §21.2(a)(2) — Other Protest Grounds

Other protests shall be filed not later than 10 calendar days after the basis of protest is known or should have been known, whichever is earlier.
